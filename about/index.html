Deities of Death – Age of Mythology Retold

Founded in 2005, Deities of Death (DoD) is a legendary Age of Mythology team with over 600 members. We are known for our competitive dominance, deep strategic knowledge, and active community involvement.

Our team provides detailed build orders, guides, and strategies updated weekly. Members can access exclusive coaching sessions, join community events, and collaborate through our vibrant Discord server.

On our Build Orders page, players will find curated strategies for each god, tailored to different matchups and maps. These guides are updated regularly based on meta shifts and competitive feedback.

The Coaching section features experienced players ranging from 1500 to over 2000 ELO, offering 1-on-1 training sessions to help you climb the ladder efficiently and improve your game sense.

We maintain an active presence on Liquipedia, YouTube, and Twitch. Our YouTube channel publishes weekly content including gameplay breakdowns, coaching replays, and community spotlights. Our Twitch streams feature live ranked games, viewer coaching, and team events.

Our Discord community is the heart of DoD, with over 600 members sharing tips, memes, feedback, and hosting casual and ranked games daily. All players are welcome, from casuals to top-tier pros.

If you're interested in becoming part of an elite, supportive, and active Age of Mythology community, DoD is the place to be.
